About the Team

We are an all women's team with about 27 members. Our club competes in USA Ultimate (USAU) tournaments. We practice and compete year round. Fall is the developmental season and spring is our competitive season. In the fall we compete in about three tournaments and in the spring we compete in about four to five tournaments.

There are tryouts at the beginning of the fall semester that last two weeks. No prior experience is required. Follow our Instagram for relevant information or contact us via Instagram or email with any questions.

Another good place to meet us and learn more about the club in person is student org night which is put on by the university at the beginning of fall semester

We practice four days a week with each practice lasting two hours. Practices are required, unless there is a conflict with class related activities. In the spring, we do have practice players who are not required to attend every practice, but they do not attend tournaments.

Dues are typically $80 per semester. This might vary depending on the semester. A large part of dues goes towards covering tournament fees and hotel prices when we travel for tournaments. The rest gets distributed to the club for different events, social things, or other expenses that may arise

Additionally, to play in sanctioned tournaments, everyone on the roster must have a USAU membership which also does cost money. The price depends on the membership that you choose to buy.

USAU Membership Information

Lastly, we are required to have a dark and a light uniform, but you only have to buy it once when you join the team. The cost usually ends up being around $100.
